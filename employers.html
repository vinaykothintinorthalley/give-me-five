Employers

Overview

Give Me Five is dedicated to supporting organizations in promoting the mental well-being of their employees. Our platform offers a comprehensive screening and intervention tool designed to address the mental health challenges faced by employees in the workplace.

Learn more

Benefits

Early Intervention

Detect mental health issues early among employees, allowing for timely intervention and support to address challenges before they impact performance and well-being.

Customized Support

Tailor interventions based on individual employee needs, ensuring that support strategies are effective and aligned with each person's unique circumstances.

Positive Work Environment

By prioritizing employee mental health, organizations can create a supportive and inclusive workplace culture where employees feel valued and supported in their mental well-being.

Implementation

Incorporating Give Me Five into mental health support services is effortless and intuitive. We offer extensive training and continuous support to help organizations effectively employ the platform in assisting individuals with managing their mental well-being.

Early Needs Identification

Use Give Me Five's simple questions to identify employees' needs early, addressing various wellbeing issues proactively.

Confidential Support Channel

Give Me Five offers a private platform for employees to seek support directly, enhancing accessibility for those in emotional distress.

Comprehensive Reporting

Receive detailed reports from Give Me Five, combining self-reported data with stakeholder insights for a deeper understanding of wellbeing issues and workplace culture.

Staff Wellbeing Insights

Give Me Five identifies issues impacting staff morale and productivity, such as anxiety and job satisfaction.

Workplace Culture Overview

Obtain insights into workplace culture through Give Me Five, enabling quick action to foster a positive environment.

Actionable Measures

Utilize Give Me Five to address workplace issues promptly and implement initiatives aimed at promoting employee wellbeing.
